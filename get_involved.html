Get Involved

There are many ways you can support the wildlife sanctuary and make a difference. Whether through donations, volunteering, or raising awareness, every action helps. Here are some ways you can get involved:

Volunteer with Us

Help us with various tasks such as animal care, habitat restoration, educational outreach, and more. Our volunteer programs range from short-term opportunities to long-term commitments.

Make a Donation

Your financial contribution helps us care for the animals, maintain their habitats, and fund conservation efforts. All donations are tax-deductible and directly support our mission.

Adopt an Animal

You can symbolically adopt one of the animals at the sanctuary, helping to cover their daily care and feeding. Adoption packages include photos, updates, and visitation privileges.

Spread the Word

Help raise awareness about the importance of wildlife conservation by sharing our mission and activities with your friends and family. Follow us on social media and help amplify our message.

For more information about how you can support our sanctuary, please contact our outreach coordinator at volunteer@wildlifesanctuary.org or call +254 700 000000.
